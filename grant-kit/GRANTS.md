(free cloud/AI credits). Each row links to its submission-ready draft. Drafts in `private/` are written
and ready; `government/` and `techno/` drafts are owned by other agents — expected file paths are listed
so the index is complete the moment they land.

> **What this kit produces:** submission-ready *drafts only*. Actually creating accounts, signing, and
> submitting stays with Maksym (his identity, his commitments). Always re-verify the live cycle and
> eligibility on the funder's site before submitting — cycles and figures move.

Legend — **Type:** gov = government · priv = private/philanthropic · tech = techno (credits).
**Fit:** High = strong, actionable alignment · Med = real but indirect (vendor-via-city, research
partner, or eligibility caveat). **Status:** "Draft ready" = narrative written here; "Draft pending
(<agent>)" = owned elsewhere, path reserved.

## Recommended sequence (by deadline + ease + value)

Prioritized for a solo operator with a live product but no external commitments yet. **Do the techno
credits first** — they are self-serve, near-certain, and immediately useful (they pay for the
infrastructure CityPulse already runs on); then the philanthropic civic-tech grants on live cycles;
gov grants only via a city/state/municipality partner.

**Tier 1 — Do now (high ease, high certainty, directly useful credits):**
1. **Microsoft for Startups Founders Hub** — $25K Azure/OpenAI, **no VC required**, fastest self-serve win.
2. **AWS Activate** (Founders self-serve now; pursue $100K+ tier if an accelerator/VC relationship lands).
3. **Vercel for Startups + Neon** — we deploy on Vercel today; up to $30K + $100K Postgres directly offsets run cost.
4. **Anthropic Startup Program** — $1K–$5K Claude credits self-serve; powers the AI-agent layer.
5. **Google for Startups Cloud** — largest credit ceiling (up to $350K) if a partner/accelerator path opens.

**Tier 2 — Apply on the next live cycle (high fit, real cash, watch deadlines):**
6. **Mozilla Democracy x AI Cohort** — best philanthropic fit ("government data infrastructure");
   $50K→$300K. *Action: confirm next cohort window (recent deadline Mar 16, 2026); commit the open-source civic core.*

   *Action: confirm next KCC window (last Apr 1–30, 2026) and whether target pilot city is a Knight city.*

**Tier 3 — Partner-dependent (start the relationship now, submit when partner is secured):**
8. **FEMA BRIC** — **open now through Jul 23, 2026**, but the prime applicant must be a **state/local
   government**. *Action: line up a municipal/state partner to apply with CityPulse as the technology/sub.*
9. **Infrastructure Canada / FCM Green Municipal Fund** — windows Apr–Jul and Oct–Dec 2026; needs a
   **Canadian municipality** prime. (Natural given existing Canada-focused projects in the portfolio.)

    *with* CityPulse, and position for What Works Cities certification support.

    civic-tech / public-policy lab** on an applied-AI RFP, contributing CityPulse as the testbed.

**Tier 4 — Watch / opportunistic:**

    only with an EU city/partner. Deadline Oct 8, 2026.
13. **EPA** — verify the live FY26 NOFO and a community/city co-applicant before investing effort.
