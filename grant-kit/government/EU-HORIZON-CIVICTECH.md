## 1. Problem Statement

European cities are on the front line of the climate crisis — heatwaves, urban flooding, drought, and storm damage — while bound by ambitious climate-neutrality and adaptation commitments. Yet the gap between **adaptation plans** and **adaptation action on the ground** remains wide:

- Cities hold fragmented data (assets, sensors, hazard maps, citizen reports) that is rarely fused into a **live, decision-ready picture** of climate risk to urban infrastructure.
- Adaptation investment is hard to **prioritize across competing needs** and harder to direct **equitably** toward the most vulnerable urban populations.
- Solutions piloted in one city rarely **transfer** to others, slowing the EU-wide scaling the Missions are designed to accelerate.
- Citizens are too often **objects** of adaptation rather than active participants generating ground-truth and legitimacy.

Horizon's Missions exist precisely to close these gaps — to move from planning to **deployed, replicable, citizen-engaged adaptation** across many cities at once. That requires a shared digital backbone.

## 2. Project Description

**CityPulse** is an AI civic-infrastructure platform proposed as the **digital adaptation backbone** for a multi-city consortium. It delivers:

1. **Climate-risk intelligence for urban infrastructure** — fuses asset, sensor, hazard, and citizen data to forecast risk from flooding, heat, and extreme weather, and to identify the most at-risk assets and neighborhoods.
2. **Equity-aware adaptation prioritization** — ranks adaptation actions by both infrastructure risk and social vulnerability, ensuring benefits reach the most exposed residents (a Mission cross-cutting priority).
3. **Predictive maintenance** — protects existing urban infrastructure and supports circular, efficient asset use by extending useful life and avoiding waste.
4. **Citizen 311 engagement** — residents report local climate impacts, generating ground-truth and participatory legitimacy.
5. **Cross-city replicability** — a common platform validated in multiple lighthouse cities, producing transferable methods, open results, and a blueprint other EU cities can adopt — directly serving the Missions' scaling mandate.

**Live demo:** https://citypulse-beta.vercel.app
**Illustrative impact (pilot-modeled):** **€-equivalent ~€3.9M/yr** (illustrative **$4.2M/yr**) in avoided cost and **82% of failures pre-detected** before service interruption — the deployable, measurable adaptation outcome Horizon Missions reward.

## 4. Public Benefit (European public value)

- **More resilient European cities**, with infrastructure protected against intensifying climate hazards.
- **Equitable adaptation** that reaches the most vulnerable urban residents across Member States.
- **A replicable blueprint** that accelerates adaptation far beyond the pilot cities — multiplying EU public value.
- **Empowered citizens** participating directly in their cities' climate response.

## 5. Climate & Equity Framing

CityPulse is a **climate-adaptation technology** at its core, aligned with Horizon's dedication of the majority-share of climate funding to action on the ground. It advances the EU's just-transition and leave-no-one-behind principles by embedding **equity-aware prioritization** so that adaptation investment reaches the urban communities most exposed to climate harm. By extending asset life and reducing waste, it also supports **circular-economy** and resource-efficiency objectives across the Missions and Cluster 4/5/6 topics.

## 9. Sustainability

Beyond the project, CityPulse persists as a **commercial SaaS** with a defined municipal business model, so deploying cities sustain the platform from operating budgets — justified by documented avoided costs. The **replication blueprint and open methods** let follower cities adopt the approach with or without the platform, multiplying impact across the EU. Consortium research partners sustain the scientific outputs; cities embed CityPulse in their statutory adaptation and asset-management plans. This combination of a viable commercial model **plus** open, transferable knowledge is exactly the durable, scalable impact Horizon Missions are designed to leave behind.
